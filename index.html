Who We Are

Test-a-psychic is the first and only website that offers the opportunity for new readers to start their career, as well as include concrete clients in the process. Test-a-psychic is a service that provides Live chat, email and phone readings for registered members (client/testers) to "test" new potential Psychic Readers and Life Coaches.

Why Us

You may wonder why bother to test potential readers through Test-a-psychic when you can easily have professional readings at regular psychic web sites. You are right to think that way. But Test-a-psychic also offers professional readings. Just because these readers are potential ones to be hired, does not undermine their abilities. We are trying to create a space that helps gifted psychics to be found. Not only these tested readers can benefit from this experience, but the same goes for you. Test-a-psychic will give you the opportunity to not only pick the gifted readers, but also do so at cheap prices. It is basically a win-win situation.
